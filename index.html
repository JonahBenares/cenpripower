Our History and Corporate Structure

CENPRI was established by Energreen Power Inter-Island Corp. to be the project company for its 15-year, 18.9MW Peak and Reserve power supply services contract with the Central Negros Electric Cooperative (CENECO).

Energreen and CENPRI are looking to be a long term provider of power grid reliability services to the fast growing economy of Bacolod and its adjoining Cities of Silay, Talisay, Bago, and Municipality of Murcia and Salvador Benedicto.Energreen aims to deliver on its winning service commitment to its customers through a strong team of well-trained and committed professionals, most of whom are from the talent pool in Central Negros.
