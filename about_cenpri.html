Central Negros Power Reliability, Inc. (CENPRI)

CENPRI was established by Energreen Power Inter-Island Corp. to be the project company for its 15-year, 18.9MW Peak and Reserve power supply services contract with the Central Negros Electric Cooperative (CENECO).

The Project will improve power reliability in CENECO’s Service area by providing supplementary power supply during peaking times and emergencies when there are shortages and grid problems in the Visayas. It is also intended to reduce CENECO’S total dependence on the Wholesale Electricity Spot Market which spikes to as much as P32 per kwh. CENECO is protected by its contracted price with CENPRI which is P6.80 to P10 per kwh depending on diesel prices. Reliability also comes from its close proximity to Bacolod that avoids the long transmission lines in the Visayas that can be congested or constrained, and cause power interruptions.

CENPRI has five (5) engines totalling a net capacity of 25MW at its plant located in Brgy Calumangan, Bago City, Negros Occidental, about 16 kilometers south of Bacolod City. It operates 24/7 and is connected to the Visayan Grid through the 69kv San Enrique transmission line of the National Grid Corp of the Philippines. The Plant uses waste heat recovery systems for better fuel efficiency and environmental compliance.

Energreen Power is a specialist in providing niche power generation services in the islands with power plants in Oriental Mindoro, Palawan, and Catanduanes. It has a business services office in Bacolod City for customer, finance, and procurement support activities.

CENPRI has a grid connection agreement with NGCP, is a registered market participant in the Visayan Wholesale Electricity Spot Market, and is duly approved by the DENR and ERC. It is connected to the Visayan Grid with redundant communications systems and emergency power.

Energreen and CENPRI are looking to be a long term provider of power grid reliability services to the fast growing economy of Bacolod and its adjoining Cities of Silay, Talisay, Bago, and Municipality of Murcia, and aim to deliver on its winning service commitment to its customers through a strong team of well-trained and committed professionals, most of whom are from the talent pool in Central Negros.

Mission

Our mission is to be the most reputable power generator and power mix designer that electric cooperatives in the off-grid islands of the Philippines aspire to work with. We are passionate and forward-thinking professionals who strive to offer the fair prices customers deserve by continuously developing hybrid technology and engaging in efficient and worthwhile projects, growing financially on a foundation of dignity, proficiency, and good corporate citizenship as a grid solutions provider.

Vision

Energreen is seeking to contribute to the socio-economic development of the islands by providing cleaner and cheaper power services and opportunities for careers and livelihoods in the areas it will serve.
